Official Rules & Regulations

1

One Account per Person

Each user is allowed only one account linked to their mobile number. Duplicate accounts will be permanently banned.

2

Prediction Deadline

Predictions must be submitted or edited exactly 30 minutes before the official kickoff time. No changes are permitted thereafter.

3

Point System

  • Correct Winner/Draw: +10 pts
  • Correct Goal Difference: +8 pts
  • Exact Score Prediction: +25 pts
4

Final Decisions

Admin decisions regarding point allocations, match results, and dispute resolutions are final and binding.

5

Fair Play

Any form of cheating, exploitation of bugs, or referral abuse will lead to immediate account suspension and disqualification from prizes.
